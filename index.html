OJT Project · Sun Life Canada Philippines

MRU FA Roadmap

A web application developed during my 486-hour practicum at Sun Life Canada Philippines Inc. under the Mount Rushmore Unit. The system guides Financial Advisors through a structured 7-level certification roadmap — covering basic requirements, Sun Life training, IC/IIAP exam review, mock exams, licensure, CA forms, and contract signing. Each level tracks step-by-step progress, and completing all requirements in Level 7 marks a Financial Advisor as fully coded — officially onboarded into the unit. Admins can monitor all FAs, manage approvals, and track overall unit progress in real time.
